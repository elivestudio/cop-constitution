SALUTATION



Salutation by officiating minister. The officiant, standing in front of the congregation, shall pronounce one of the following salutations:

a) Grace, mercy and peace from God the Father and from Jesus Christ, the son, be with us in truth and love (2 Jn. 3).

b) Grace and peace to you from God the Father and from the Lord Jesus Christ (2 Cor. 1:2).

c) The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, and the love of God, and the fellowship of the Holy Spirit be with you all (2 Cor. 13:14).
