Sunday Morning

It is the tradition of The Church of Pentecost that, as soon as a member enters the church building, that member sits down and prays fervently, giving thanks to the Lord, glorifying Him, etc., until the officiating minister (or his representative) opens the service. The service may follow this pattern:

1 Opening prayer

2 Salutation by officiant:

The officiant, standing in front of the congregation, shall pronounce one of the salutations described in 6 above:

1 Songs of praise by the congregation

2 Testimonies, individual Bible readings, song ministration, etc

3. Call to worship

4. Message and prayer

5. Altar call

6. Tithes and offerings

7. Announcements

8. Prayer and benediction (prayer may be said for the nation and rulers in accordance with 1 Timothy 2:1-3 and Romans. 13:1-7.
