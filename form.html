ABOUT

The C.O.P. Constitution 1.0.0.3 is a companion for leaders in the Church of Pentecost and the general public who desire to learn more about this Pentecostal Church. The C.O.P originated from the ministry of the late Rev. James McKeown who live and ministered among ideginoues of Asamankese, Akroso, Winniba, Cape Coast and later served as the chairman of the Church.

The primary aims and objectives of this great church is to preach and propagate what was commissioned by Christ. The Constitution guides users on how the church intends to carry on the teachings of Christ.

This app presents word to word library as updated in the year 2016. The constitution constains 38 articles grouped in 8 chapters. The navigation right in the app lists the constitution chapter by chapter.

The landing page of the app dispays data table with search form. Other resources available in the app could be easily found in the search form or by a scroll down.
